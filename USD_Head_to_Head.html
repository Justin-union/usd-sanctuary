Collections Projection (Conservative Baseline + Upside Case)

These figures use a conservative projection baseline, informed by the USD improvements above: stronger upfront coverage, fewer accounts sent to collections, and lower remaining balances per account.

Conservative baseline: projected collections recovery declines from $22,902 (eDeposit) to $12,545 (USD), because more balances are resolved earlier via deposit recovery before reaching collections.

Potential upside: with only 30 accounts left for follow-up (vs 82), collections effort can be more concentrated, which may improve realized collection percentage versus this baseline assumption.
